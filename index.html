DubAI

DubAI is a user-friendly tool that lets you take a video in any foreign language and instantly convert it into an English version without awkward dubbing. You upload your video, and it automatically:
1. Transcribes the original speech,
2. Translates it into English,
3. Recreates the speech using the actor's voice, and
4. Synchronizes speaker's lips to match the new voice.
The end result is a natural-looking, English-speaking version of your video that sounds and feels genuine - no more out-of-sync or robotic dubbing.

CHATLAS

CHATLAS (An Atlas for Websites) is an intelligent chatbot that turns any website into a conversational assistant. It starts by using the site's sitemap to automatically gather information from all its pages, and then allows users to ask natural-language questions based on that content.
Whether it's a government site, policy portal, or product documentation hub, BARAC makes it easy to get clear, accurate answers without having to search manually - acting like a personalized help desk for the entire website.
