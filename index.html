Dub and lip-sync any video

DubAI is a user-friendly tool that lets you take a video in any foreign language and instantly convert it into an English version without awkward dubbing. You upload your video, and it automatically:
1. Transcribes the original speech,
2. Translates it into English,
3. Recreates the speech using the actor's voice, and
4. Synchronizes speaker's lips to match the new voice.
The end result is a natural-looking, English-speaking version of your video that sounds and feels genuine - no more out-of-sync or robotic dubbing.

Robo Investors with ML

A comprehensive AI-powered investment management system that democratizes algorithmic trading and portfolio optimization typically reserved for hedge funds. This intelligent robo-advisor leverages machine learning and statistical modeling to provide personalized investment recommendations:
Risk Assessment: Uses Random Forest algorithms to analyze market risk factors and predict interest rate movements
Stock Classification: Employs XGBoost models to classify 3,000+ stocks into investment-worthy categories
Price Forecasting: Individual LSTM neural networks for each stock to predict future performance and price movements
Portfolio Optimization: Custom loss functions integrate risk predictions, classifications, and forecasts to generate personalized investment allocations across asset baskets with specific recommended amounts for each stock.
